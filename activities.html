Yosemite

Fall 2024

Yosemite- Fall 2024

Explored the Valley with Dad and learned how to fly fish in Lyell Canyon, on the eastern side of the park. Caught several small to medium-sized brook trout. First time casting in the backcountry.

Sequoia/Kings Canyon

Spring 2024

Sequoia/Kings Canyon- Spring 2024

Solo backpacked for a week in SEKI. Covered over 60 miles and 3,600M of elevation. Summited Twin Peaks (3,135M), confronted an adult black bear, and bushwhacked for two days to bypass an overflowing creek/snowed-out pass. First time solo in the backcountry.

Socialize- Summer 2023

Left work for three-months to design, build, and launch a closed beta for Socialize- a platform that connects users in-person by curating a feed of all the local social venues and events a user’s friends actively want to go to. First time launching on TestFlight.

Red Rock Canyon

Winter 2023

Red Rock Canyon- Winter 2023

Climbed for a week in Red Rock Canyon, Nevada. Tagged peaks when the rock was too wet to climb. Led the Woodsman (5.9, 220’) and single-pitch sport routes up to 5.11a. First time night climbing and leading a multi-pitch sport route.

Excavated a section of Hadrian’s Villa (Villa Adriana), a UNESCO World Heritage Site outside of Tivoli, Italy. Learned about archaeological fieldwork and recovered Roman artifacts, from pottery to glassware, not seen for thousands of years.
